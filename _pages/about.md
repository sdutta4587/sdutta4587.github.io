I am a Research Assistant at the [Indian Institute of Sciences, Bangalore]{https://iisc.ac.in/} working with [Prof. Rajesh Chaunsali]{https://rajeshchaunsali.github.io/}. My research is focused on firstly, developing an experimental setup to realise periodically time varying stiffness in a pendulum system and secondly, studying the Non-reciprocal/Non-hermitian effect on stiffness and damper of a periodic lattice. 

Prior to my research assistantship, I was an undergraduate student in the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) program at [NIT Silchar]{http://www.nits.ac.in/}. My bachelor's thesis involved studying and analyzing static and dynamical behaviour of a Laminated composite plate under thermal effects under the guidance of [Prof. Atanu Sahu]{http://ce.nits.ac.in/atanu/}. In the final semester, I was selected as an Undergraduate Research Student in a fully funded project under Undergraduate Research Council (UGRC) at [NIT Silchar]{http://www.nits.ac.in/} to study the design optimization of low cost Bamboo towers. In the final year of my Undergraduate studies, I was able to secure AA grade in my both bachelors project I and II due to my exceptional problem solving ability.
